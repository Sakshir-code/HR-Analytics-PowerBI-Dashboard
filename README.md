# HR-Analytics-PowerBI-Dashboard
Interactive HR Analytics Dashboard built in Power BI to analyze workforce distribution, attrition trends, employee performance, salary insights, and workforce planning metrics across departments, job roles, countries, and work modes.

# About the Project
This project was created to explore workforce and employee-related data through an interactive Power BI dashboard. The goal was to understand employee distribution, attrition patterns, performance trends, and salary insights in a way that helps HR teams and business leaders make better decisions.
Instead of looking at raw data and spreadsheets, this dashboard provides a clear view of key workforce metrics through interactive visualizations and filters.
## Dataset
This project uses a subset of a publicly available HR dataset from Kaggle.

The original dataset contains approximately 1 million records. For this analysis, I worked with around 200,000 employee records to focus on dashboard performance and business analysis.

Dataset Source:
https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/rohitgrewal/hr-data-mnc

## Why I Built This Dashboard
Managing a large workforce comes with several challenges such as employee attrition, workforce planning, performance monitoring, and understanding salary distribution across departments.

## I built this dashboard to answer questions like:

1.Which departments have the highest employee count?
2.Which job roles experience the most attrition?
3.How has attrition changed over time?
4.Is there any relationship between employee performance and attrition?
5.How are employees distributed across departments, countries, and work modes?

# Business Solution
This dashboard was developed to provide a centralized view of workforce data and help HR teams make informed decisions based on employee trends and performance metrics.
## The solution enables stakeholders to:

1.Monitor workforce health through key metrics such as employee count, attrition rate, active employees, and average salary.
2.Identify departments and job roles with higher attrition rates to support employee retention initiatives.
3.Analyze workforce distribution across departments, countries, and experience levels for better resource planning.
4.Evaluate employee performance trends and compare ratings across different departments.
5.Understand the impact of work modes (Remote, Hybrid, and On-site) on employee engagement and attrition.
6.Track workforce trends over time to support long-term strategic planning.
7.Enable data-driven HR decision-making through interactive filters, drill-down analysis, and visual reporting

# Business Value Delivered

By transforming raw workforce data into actionable insights, this dashboard helps organizations:

1.Improve employee retention strategies
2.Support workforce planning and resource allocation
3.Identify potential attrition risks early
4.Monitor organizational performance more effectively
5.Reduce manual reporting efforts through automated visual analytics

# Key Insights

Some interesting observations from the analysis include:

1.The IT department has the largest workforce and salary expenditure.
2.Certain job roles contribute more heavily to overall attrition.
3.Attrition trends have shown noticeable changes over the years.
4.Employee distribution varies significantly across departments and countries.
5.Performance ratings remain relatively balanced across most departments
